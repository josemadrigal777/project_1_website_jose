Who is Optic Pamaj?

Optic Pamaj, also known as Austin Pamajewon, is a proffesional gamer. He is better known as Pamaj. He was born in Canada. He was born on March 7, 1996. He is 20 years old.

He has a Youtube channel and a total of 3.2 million subsrcibers. He started his youtube channel on October 8th, 2010. He is in a competitive esports team Optic Gaming. He is also a proffesional sniper in Call of Duty. He got recruited for Optic Gaming on November 3, 2015.
